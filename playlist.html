The Beats

Welcome to Regulated Beats, your ultimate destination for the best old-school West Coast hip-hop and RnB. Founded in 2025 and broadcasting from Medicine Hat, Alberta, Canada, we bring you the sounds that defined an era. From the smooth G-Funk grooves of Dr. Dre to the raw lyricism of 2Pac, Ice Cube, Eazy-E, and E-40, our station celebrates the timeless beats and authentic culture of West Coast music. We pay special tribute to the legendary Death Row Records, the iconic label that helped shape the sound of the '90s and launched the careers of some of hip-hop’s biggest names. Along with influential soundtracks like Above The Rim and Juice, we highlight the groundbreaking albums and tracks that came from Death Row, forever impacting the genre. With a curated playlist of over 10,000 songs, Regulated Beats is your go-to station for a vast collection of tracks that showcase the golden era of West Coast hip-hop and RnB. Whether you're a longtime fan or just discovering the roots of this legendary sound, we’ve got the music that continues to shape and inspire. Tune in, stay connected, and immerse yourself in the vibe that never fades. This is Regulated Beats..
